SHADER7 FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Are SHADER7 tools really free forever?
Yes. Every tool is 100% free with no limits, no premium tiers, and no registration. We sustain through voluntary donations and non-intrusive ads.
How do you make money if everything is free?
Google AdSense displays relevant ads, and users can donate via Stripe. This covers server costs while keeping tools free for everyone.
Is my data safe? Do you store my photos or salary information?
No data is stored. All processing happens in your browser (client-side JavaScript). Close the tab and your data is permanently deleted from memory.
Do I need to create an account?
Never. We don't offer user accounts—no passwords to remember, no emails required.
What countries do your passport photos support?
150+ countries including US, UK, Canada, India, all Schengen countries, Australia, Japan, and more. Each meets official biometric requirements.
How accurate is the salary calculator?
Calculates gross pay precisely. Tax estimates are based on 2025 federal/state brackets and accurate within ±2% for most US/UK/Canada scenarios.
Can I use these tools for my business?
Yes. Commercial use is permitted under our Terms. Many small businesses use our receipt generator and salary calculator.
Do you have a mobile app?
Not yet, but the website is fully mobile-responsive. Tools work perfectly on phones and tablets.
Who created SHADER7?
Nishikant Xalxo, a CNC milling operator and web developer from India. He built the tools to solve real problems in manufacturing and finance.
What is the "PRO" version of passport photo tool?
It's a version with batch processing and advanced editing. Still completely free—no login or payment required.
How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Use our contact form. We typically respond within 24 hours and prioritize bug fixes.
Are the CNC machinist tools suitable for beginners?
Yes. Includes basic G-code references for hobbyists and advanced calculators for professionals. All skill levels covered.
Can I donate to support the project?
Absolutely! Donations via Stripe help cover server costs. Every contribution directly supports keeping tools free.
What file formats does the receipt generator support?
Export as PDF, PNG, or JPG. All formats maintain professional quality suitable for business use.
Is there an API for developers?
Not currently. All tools are designed for direct browser use. API access may be offered in future based on demand.
How often are tools updated?
Major updates quarterly. Tax calculator updated immediately when new brackets release. Bug fixes within 48 hours.
Do you collect IP addresses?
No. We use privacy-focused Plausible Analytics which anonymizes IPs. No server logs store IP addresses.
Can I share or embed your tools on my website?
You may link to our tools. Direct embedding is prohibited by our Terms to maintain security and user privacy.
What if I need help using a tool?
Each tool page includes step-by-step instructions. Blog has detailed tutorials. Contact form for specific questions.
Are you compliant with Google AdSense policies?
Yes. We're fully compliant with original, expert content, clear policies, and no deceptive practices. Approval is our goal.
