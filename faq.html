Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are the tools on SHADER7 really free to use?

Yes, absolutely. All tools and applications available on SHADER7.com are 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or charges. The project is supported by advertising, which allows us to keep the tools free for everyone.

2. Do you store my personal data?

Your privacy is a top priority. For tools like the Passport Photo Creator and Resume Maker, all processing is done within your browser. We do not upload, save, or view any of the personal files or information you use in our applications. For more details, please read our full Privacy Policy.

3. How does the Passport Photo Creator work?

The Passport Photo Creator allows you to upload a photo, select the correct dimensions for your country's requirements, and adjust the crop. You can then arrange multiple copies onto a single, standard-sized sheet (like A4 or Letter) to make printing easy and cost-effective.

4. Is the Resume Maker suitable for professions other than Mechanical Engineering?

While the Resume Maker was initially designed with mechanical engineers in mind, its structure and formatting are versatile. Anyone looking to create a clean, professional resume can use it effectively. Simply tailor the content to your specific industry and experience.

5. I found a bug or have a suggestion. How can I contact you?

We appreciate your feedback! If you encounter an issue, have a suggestion for a new feature, or want to recommend a new tool, please send an email to nxdecore@gmail.com. We read every message and are always looking for ways to improve the site.
