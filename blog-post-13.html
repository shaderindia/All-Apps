Professional Receipt Requirements: Tax Compliance

By Nishikant Xalxo | Tax Compliance Expert | Updated: January 10, 2025 | 9 min read

After my small business was audited over improper receipts, I learned what makes them legally valid. Whether you're a freelancer, shop owner, or just need proper records, here's what receipts must include.

Required Fields (All Countries)

Mandatory for legal validity:
  • Business name and address
  • Date of transaction
  • Itemized list of goods/services
  • Unit price and quantity
  • Total amount
  • Tax amount (if applicable)
  • Payment method

Country-Specific Requirements

Country Special Requirements Tax Number
United States None (receipts not legally required, but recommended for taxes) EIN if business
India GSTIN mandatory for GST-registered businesses GSTIN (15 digits)
United Kingdom VAT number for VAT-registered businesses VAT number (9 digits)
EU Countries VAT number mandatory for B2B transactions Country code + VAT

India GST Receipt Rules

For GST-registered businesses (turnover > ₹20 lakh):

  • GSTIN must be on receipt
  • Separate CGST, SGST, or IGST amounts
  • HSN code for goods (4 digits)
  • SAC code for services (6 digits)
  • Place of supply
Sample GST Receipt (India): 
-----------------------------------------
    XYZ ENGINEERING
    123 Industrial Area, Mumbai
    GSTIN: 27ABCDE1234F1Z5
-----------------------------------------
Date: 15-Jan-2025
Invoice No: INV-2025-0015

Item          Qty  Rate     Amount
CNC Service    1   ₹5,000   ₹5,000

Subtotal: ₹5,000
CGST (9%): ₹450
SGST (9%): ₹450
Total: ₹5,900

Payment: Bank Transfer
-----------------------------------------

US Receipt Best Practices

While not legally required for most transactions, receipts are essential for:

  • Tax deductions (business expenses)
  • Warranty claims
  • Expense reimbursements
  • Returns/exchanges

IRS Requirements for Business Deductions:

  • Date
  • Amount
  • Business purpose
  • Vendor name
  • Items purchased

UK VAT Receipt Rules

For VAT-registered businesses (turnover > £85,000):

  • VAT registration number
  • Date of supply
  • Description of goods/services
  • Total excluding VAT
  • VAT rate and amount
  • Total including VAT

Digital Receipts

Electronic receipts are valid if:

  • Sent from verifiable business email
  • Contains all required fields
  • Can be printed (PDF format)
  • Timestamped
Warning: Screenshots of text messages or emails without business headers are NOT valid receipts for tax purposes.

Receipt Retention Periods

United States 3 years (7 years for capital assets)
India 6 years from end of assessment year
United Kingdom 6 years
