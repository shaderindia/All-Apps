How to Calculate Overtime Pay: US & India Laws

By Nishikant Xalxo | Salary Calculation Expert | Updated: January 10, 2025 | 11 min read

Overtime pay calculation seems simple—1.5x rate—but state laws, exemptions, and daily/weekly thresholds make it complex. I've built salary calculators for both US and Indian payroll systems. Here's the breakdown.

United States Overtime Laws (FLSA)

Federal Standard (Most States)

  • 40 hours/week: Overtime threshold
  • 1.5x rate: Time-and-a-half for hours over 40
  • No daily OT: Can work 12 hours in a day with no OT (unless state says otherwise)
  • Exempt employees: Salaried managers, professionals don't get OT
Federal OT Calculation Example: 
Rate: $20/hour
Hours worked: 48 hours
Regular pay: 40 × $20 = $800
OT pay: 8 × ($20 × 1.5) = $240
Total: $800 + $240 = $1,040

State Variations

State Daily OT Threshold Special Rules
California 8 hours Double time after 12 hours
Alaska 8 hours 1.5x after 8h, 2x after 12h
Nevada 8 hours (if < $12/hr) Applies to lower-wage workers only
Colorado 12 hours 1.5x after 12h, after 40h/week
Texas/Florida None Follow federal 40h/week only
California Example (Most Complex): 
Rate: $25/hour, Hours: 14 hours

First 8 hours: 8 × $25 = $200
Hours 8-12: 4 × ($25 × 1.5) = $150
Hours 12-14: 2 × ($25 × 2) = $100
Total: $200 + $150 + $100 = $450 (for 14-hour day)

India Overtime Laws (Factories Act, 1948)

Indian overtime law is simpler but has daily and weekly components:

  • 9 hours/day: Maximum normal working hours
  • 48 hours/week: Maximum normal weekly hours
  • 2x rate: Double wages for overtime hours
  • 12 hours/day max: Including OT (factory workers)
India OT Calculation: 
Rate: ₹500/day (8 hours = ₹62.5/hour)
Hours worked: 10 hours

Normal: 8 hours = ₹500
OT: 2 hours × ₹125/hour (double rate) = ₹250
Total: ₹500 + ₹250 = ₹750

Common Employer Violations

1. Misclassifying employees as exempt: Calling workers "managers" to avoid OT pay. Must meet salary AND duties tests.
2. Averaging hours across weeks: Cannot average 35h + 45h weeks to avoid OT. OT calculated weekly.
3. Not paying OT on bonuses: OT rate must include shift differentials and non-discretionary bonuses.
4. Illegal in India: Not paying double rate or forcing >12h days without compensation.

Calculating OT with Shift Differentials

If you earn night shift premium, OT rate must include it:

Example: 
Base rate: $20/hour
Night premium: +$3/hour
Effective rate: $23/hour
OT rate: $23 × 1.5 = $34.50/hour

10 OT hours: 10 × $34.50 = $345 OT pay
