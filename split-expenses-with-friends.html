Fair Expense Splitting With Friends

Whether you're organizing a group vacation, sharing an apartment, or just splitting a large dinner bill, money can quickly make things awkward among friends. Fair expense splitting is about transparency and simplicity.

Why Manual Splitting Fails

Trying to keep track of who paid for what in a notes app usually leads to forgotten expenses and resentment. When "Person A" pays for the hotel and "Person B" pays for dinner, figuring out who owes whom requires a centralized system.

Best Practices

  • Log it immediately: Enter expenses as soon as they happen.
  • Keep receipts: Transparency prevents disputes.
  • Settle up regularly: Don't let debts linger for months.
