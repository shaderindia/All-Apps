Salary Calculator FAQ

How is my hourly rate calculated?

Your base salary is divided by fixed base hours. Example: 4000 RON / 160 hours = 25 RON/hour.

Does weekend work pay extra?

Weekend hours are multiplied by the weekend rate you choose. Weekday hours stay at the regular hourly rate.

Where is my data stored?

Your saved monthly data is stored in your browser localStorage. It is not uploaded to a server by this page.
