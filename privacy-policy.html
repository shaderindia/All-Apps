1. Privacy-First Principle

SHADER7 operates with a privacy-first approach. We design many tools so that photos, resumes, calculations, and user inputs can be processed directly inside your browser whenever possible. We do not require users to create an account to use the main tools.

However, because this website may use third-party services such as Google AdSense, Google Translate, analytics, hosting, contact forms, or communication services, it is not accurate to say that absolutely no data is ever processed. This policy explains what may be processed and why.

3. Browser-Based Processing

Many SHADER7 tools, including photo editing, photo compression, resume building, calculators, and similar tools, are designed to work inside your browser. This means the processing can happen locally on your device without requiring account registration.

Browser-based processing helps reduce unnecessary data collection. Still, your browser, device, internet provider, hosting provider, or third-party scripts may process normal technical information needed to load and operate web pages.

4. Advertising and Google AdSense

SHADER7 may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies, device identifiers, IP address information, browser information, ad interactions, and similar technologies to provide, measure, and personalize ads depending on your settings and applicable law.

You can manage Google ad personalization and privacy choices using Google’s own advertising and privacy controls. Do not click advertisements artificially or encourage others to click them.

5. Third-Party Services

SHADER7 may use or link to third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Google AdSense: Used to display and measure advertisements.
  • Google Translate: Used to provide optional page translation features.
  • Google Fonts / Font Awesome / CDN services: Used to load fonts, icons, and interface assets.
  • GitHub Pages or hosting providers: Used to host and serve the website.
  • Form/contact services or email: Used when you contact SHADER7.
  • WebRTC, signaling, relay, or TURN services: May be used by Quick Private Chat communication features.
  • Payment or donation providers: May be used if sponsorship, donation, or payment features are offered.

These third-party providers may process data under their own privacy policies. SHADER7 does not control every technical process used by those third-party providers.

6. Quick Private Chat / BER OF CHAT

Quick Private Chat, also referred to in some pages as BER OF CHAT, is a communication feature that may use WebRTC, peer-to-peer connections, signaling, room codes, relay servers, or similar technology.

  • Message content: SHADER7 does not intend to store private chat messages as normal website content.
  • WebRTC: WebRTC traffic is encrypted in transit, but peer-to-peer networking may expose technical details such as IP addresses to peers or relay/signaling services.
  • Room codes: Room codes are used to connect users. Anyone with a valid room code may be able to join the relevant session, depending on the tool design.
  • Relay / TURN: If relay or masking features are used, traffic may pass through third-party relay infrastructure.
  • Reports: If you report abuse, the report information you submit may be received and reviewed by SHADER7.
  • Age restriction: Quick Private Chat is intended for users who are at least 19 years old.

7. Cookies, LocalStorage, and Browser Storage

SHADER7 may use browser storage such as localStorage to save tool preferences, consent choices, draft inputs, or temporary app settings on your device. This helps tools work smoothly without requiring an account.

Third-party services such as advertising, translation, analytics, or embedded assets may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies according to their own policies and your consent/settings where applicable.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have privacy rights under laws such as GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, India’s DPDP Act, or other applicable laws. These may include rights to access, correction, deletion, portability, objection, withdrawal of consent, or complaint.

  • You can clear browser-stored SHADER7 data by clearing your browser storage/cache for this site.
  • You can manage cookies and tracking preferences through your browser and third-party provider settings.
  • If you contacted us by email, you may request deletion of your email/message where legally possible.
  • You may contact us for privacy requests at nxdecore@gmail.com.

9. Children and Age Restrictions

General SHADER7 tools are intended for normal lawful use and do not require user accounts. Quick Private Chat / BER OF CHAT is intended only for users who are at least 19 years old.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data to SHADER7, contact us at nxdecore@gmail.com.

10. Data Security

SHADER7 uses HTTPS where available through its hosting setup. Browser-based processing reduces the need to send user files or inputs to a server. However, no website, browser, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

11. Data Retention

For browser-based tools, data may exist only temporarily in your browser memory or local browser storage. If you submit a contact request or email, we may retain the message as needed to respond, maintain records, prevent abuse, or comply with legal obligations.

Third-party services may retain technical, advertising, translation, hosting, or communication data according to their own policies.

12. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the website, tools, advertising setup, or legal requirements change. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
