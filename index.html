About Shader7

Free Browser Tools for Daily Work

Shader7 is a collection of free online tools made for practical everyday tasks. You can create passport photos, compress images, build resumes, calculate salary, generate receipts, split bills, and explore CNC machining resources from one simple homepage.

Many Shader7 tools are designed to work directly inside your browser, so users can complete tasks quickly without creating an account. The goal is to keep useful digital tools simple, accessible, and beginner-friendly.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Shader7 free to use?

Yes. Shader7 tools are free to use and do not require account registration.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Most tools work directly in your browser on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

Many tools are browser-based and process files locally. Always check each tool page for its privacy notes.

Who made Shader7?

Shader7 is developed by Nishikant Xalxo and shared as a collection of useful free tools.
