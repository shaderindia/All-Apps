Hourly to Yearly Salary Guide

When you're offered a new job at an hourly rate, it can be tough to visualize what your actual take-home pay will look like. Understanding how to convert hourly wages to an annual salary is crucial for personal budgeting.

The Standard Formula

A standard full-time work year is generally calculated at 2,080 hours (40 hours a week × 52 weeks). To find your gross annual salary, simply multiply your hourly rate by 2,080.

Example: $25/hour × 2,080 = $52,000 per year.

Gross vs. Net Pay

Remember that your calculated total is your Gross Pay (before taxes). Your Net Pay (what hits your bank account) will be lower after federal taxes, state taxes, and benefits deductions.
