Creating Professional Receipts

Whether you are a freelance designer, a contractor, or running a small side hustle, issuing professional receipts is non-negotiable for accounting and building trust with clients.

Why Manual Receipts Are Risky

Writing receipts by hand or using basic Word templates often leads to missing information. A legally compliant receipt should always include the date, a unique invoice/receipt number, a breakdown of services, taxes, and the total amount paid.
