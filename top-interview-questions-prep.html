Top Interview Questions & Prep

Your ATS-friendly resume got you in the door. Now, you need to ace the interview. Hiring managers rely on behavioral questions to predict your future performance based on past behavior.

The STAR Method

When asked questions like "Tell me about a time you faced a challenge," always use the STAR framework:

  • S - Situation: Set the scene contextually.
  • T - Task: Describe your specific responsibility.
  • A - Action: Explain exactly what steps you took.
  • R - Result: Share the positive outcome (use numbers if possible).

Make sure the experiences you talk about in your interview match what is written on your CV. If you need to update yours, use our Free Resume Builder to align your documents.
