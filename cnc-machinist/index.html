Common Questions

How do I calculate Drilling RPM?

Use the formula: RPM = (SFM √ó 3.82) / Diameter. Or simply use our Free Drill Calculator to get the exact speed instantly.

What is a PCD in machining?

PCD stands for "Pitch Circle Diameter". It is the imaginary circle where the centers of bolt holes are located. Our calculator helps you find the X and Y coordinates for each hole.

Are these tools compatible with Fanuc?

Yes, the G-Code output from our generators is standard ISO G-Code, fully compatible with Fanuc, Haas, Mazak, and Mitsubishi controllers.
