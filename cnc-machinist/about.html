About SHADER7

Our mission is to empower machinists with free, accessible tools.

Our Story

SHADER7 was born from a simple idea: to make a machinist's life easier. The creator, **Nishikant Xalxo**, a professional with a passion for both technology and manufacturing, recognized a common problem. Machinists often rely on expensive software, outdated calculators, or time-consuming manual processes for simple but critical tasks. This not only slows down work but also increases the chance of error.

We set out to change that. SHADER7 is a commitment to the global community of machinists and programmers. We build **free, web-based tools** that are simple, fast, and accessible from any device. Our goal is to provide a reliable digital toolkit that you can carry in your pocket, making you more productive whether you're on the shop floor, in the office, or at home. What We Offer

Our platform is designed to be a comprehensive resource. We currently offer a range of **calculators and G-code utilities**, and we have many more planned. Our tools are built with precision and user-friendliness in mind, ensuring you get accurate results with minimal effort.

  • **CNC Calculators:** Get instant, precise calculations for drilling RPM, feed rates, and more.
  • **G-Code Utilities:** Tools to help you generate, format, and edit your G-code programs.
  • **Educational Resources:** We plan to introduce a blog with articles and guides to help machinists stay updated and improve their skills.
