About SHADER7

Empowering professionals with simple, free, and private online tools.

Our Mission

In a digital world filled with subscriptions, paywalls, and constant demands for your personal data, SHADER7 was created to be different. Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, professional tools that are accessible to everyone, completely free, and with an unwavering commitment to user privacy.

We believe that essential tasks like creating a resume, preparing a passport photo, or calculating your salary shouldn't be complicated or costly. Our tools are designed to be intuitive, instant, and effective, helping you get things done without any friction.

The Creator

SHADER7 was created by Nishikant Xalxo, a CNC Milling Operator and Web Developer. This site combines his passion for precision and efficiency from the world of engineering with his belief in accessible technology for everyone.
