Online Web Tools & Data Security

In an era where personal data is constantly harvested, uploading your passport photo, financial details, or personal resume to a random website can feel risky. How do you know your data is safe?

Client-Side Processing is King

At SHADER7, we prioritize your privacy. The safest web tools utilize client-side processing. This means that when you use our calculators or photo croppers, the calculations and image processing happen directly inside your web browser using JavaScript.

Your sensitive salary data, resume details, and personal photos are not sent to or stored on our servers. When you close the tab, the data is gone. Always look for tools that don't require account registration or mandatory cloud uploads to function.
