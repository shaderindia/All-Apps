Sponsorships, Partnerships, & Security Policies

Shader7 is an independent, developer-funded portal dedicated to providing free, high-performance, and privacy-first web utilities to millions of users worldwide. We welcome technical collaborations, corporate sponsorships, and developer feedback to ensure the platform remains stable, responsive, and secure. Below are our official procedures for business inquiries, security reports, and data privacy records.

1. Corporate Sponsorship & Support Models

Maintaining a high-speed, zero-downtime Progressive Web App (PWA) requires continuous hosting resources, SSL certificates, CDN caching allocations, and domain management. If your company shares our vision of open-access, zero-tracking web tools and would like to sponsor specific calculators or support the general development pipeline, please contact creator **Nishikant Xalxo** directly at nxdecore@gmail.com with the subject line [Shader7 Sponsorship Inquiry].

2. Vulnerability Disclosure & Bug Reporting

We take cybersecurity and code integrity seriously. If you identify a bug, a cross-site scripting (XSS) vulnerability, an API mismatch, or a caching loop issue within our service workers, please report it immediately:

  • Email: Send a detailed summary, including proof-of-concept steps and browser version logs, to nxdecore@gmail.com.
  • Instagram: Reach out directly to @nishix_vamp for rapid developer response on layout and UI-related bug reports.
We enforce a strict 48-hour patching policy to resolve all verified security and rendering vulnerabilities.

3. Zero-Liability Data Collection Policy

Under our strict privacy-first architecture, the Shader7 portal does not maintain backend user accounts, profile databases, or session tracking layers. We do not store or transmit:

  • Biometric face photographs from our Passport Photo tools.
  • Personal career history, contact details, or job titles from our Resume Builder.
  • Financial transaction values, invoice numbers, or business items from our Receipt Maker.
  • Chat histories, room codes, or cryptographic keys from our P2P Chat.
All variables and assets are processed locally in your browser's sandboxed virtual memory. Consequently, we cannot comply with personal data extraction or deletion requests under GDPR or CCPA, as we possess **absolutely zero user data** to extract or delete.
