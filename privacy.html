Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Last updated: August 15, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

SHADER7 ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and its tools (the "Service"). Our core principle is to collect as little information as possible.

We do not require you to create an account or register to use any of our tools.

Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be crystal clear about the data we do not collect or store:

  • Personal Information: We do not ask for, collect, or store any personal information such as your name, email address, or physical address.
  • User-Generated Content: Any data you input into our tools (like photos for the Passport Photo Creator, financial data for calculators, or text for the Resume Builder) is processed directly in your browser. This data is never sent to, or stored on, our servers. Once you close the browser tab, that information is gone unless you have saved it to your own device.

Information We Collect

To improve our website and understand how it is used, we collect anonymous, non-personal data through standard web analytics.

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous information about how the Service is accessed and used. This may include information such as your computer's browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Cookies: We use a single cookie for the sole purpose of remembering if you have accepted our Terms and Conditions pop-up. This is a functional cookie and is not used for tracking or advertising.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display ads on our website. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.
  • Stripe: We use Stripe to process voluntary donations. If you choose to donate, your payment information is handled directly by Stripe and is subject to their privacy policy. We do not see or store your credit card details.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
