A Note on Our Tools: Your Data is Yours

The most important thing to know is that our tools (Passport Photo Creator, Resume Maker, etc.) process your data entirely within your web browser on your device. We do not upload, save, view, or have any access to the files (like photos) or personal information (like resume details) you input into these tools. Your data remains on your computer.

We collect two types of information:

Cookies and Web Beacons

We use cookies to enhance your experience and to support our advertising partners.

  • A "cookie" is a small data file that is stored on your device.
  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (specifically the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads on SHADER7.com based on a user's prior visits to our site and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

You can choose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings. However, this can affect how you are able to interact with our site as well as other websites.

Children's Privacy
