Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 10, 2025

Our Core Principle: Zero Data Collection

SHADER7 ("we," "us," "our") operates on a radical privacy-first principle: we collect as little information as possible—ideally, none at all. This Privacy Policy explains exactly what we do and don't collect when you use our website and tools.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicitly clear about data we NEVER collect:

  • Personal Information: No names, emails, addresses, phone numbers
  • User Content: Photos, financial data, resumes are processed in your browser only
  • Account Data: We don't offer accounts—no passwords or profiles
  • Location Data: We don't request or store GPS locations
  • Browsing History: No tracking of pages visited or tools used

Information We Collect (Minimal)

Only two types of data may be collected:

  • Functional Cookie: A single cookie remembers if you accepted our Terms pop-up. No tracking capability.
  • Anonymized Analytics: We use Plausible Analytics (IP anonymized, no cookies) to see visitor counts only. Cannot identify individuals.

Client-Side Processing Explained

All tool processing happens in your browser using JavaScript. When you upload a photo for passport editing or enter salary data, it NEVER leaves your device. Close the tab and your data is permanently gone.

Third-Party Services

We use these external services:

  • Google AdSense: May set cookies for personalized ads. Opt-out here.
  • Stripe: Processes donations. We never see payment details.
  • Formspree: Handles contact form submissions (stores email, message only).

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Under GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California), you have rights:

  • Access: Request any data we have (likely none)
  • Deletion: We can't delete what we don't store
  • Portability: Export your own tool results
  • Opt-out: Disable cookies in your browser

Data Retention & Security

Since we don't store user data, retention isn't applicable. Our servers are hosted on AWS with industry-standard encryption. We maintain regular security audits and updates.

Children's Privacy

Our tools are suitable for all ages. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us immediately and we'll remove it.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy annually. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date. No email notifications (we don't collect emails).
