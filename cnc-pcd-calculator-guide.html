CNC PCD Calculator Guide

Machining a bolt hole circle (Pitch Circle Diameter or PCD) manually used to require extensive trigonometry and referencing sine/cosine tables. Today, a good calculator does the heavy lifting.

What You Need to Know

To calculate the exact X and Y coordinates for a bolt hole pattern, you only need three variables:

  • The center point (X0, Y0 is usually easiest).
  • The diameter of the bolt circle (PCD).
  • The total number of holes required.
